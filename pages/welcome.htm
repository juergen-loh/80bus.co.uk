Welcome

Welcome to this web site which is dedicated to all things 80-BUS.

The site acts as a repository for software, documentation, reference images and just about anything else that may be of interest to a collector or restorer of 80-BUS systems. A major focus of interest is in technical information that will help get that dust covered system working again, identify common problems and generally aid in keeping your 80-BUS system healthy and operational.
