Nascom 2 - 3A PSU

Description

Both Nascom 1 and Nascom 2 computers where supplied with the same 3A PSU kit once the original Nascom 1 design had been phased out.  The unit is fairly simple but has proved robust.  A common modification was to replace the 3A 5V regulator with a 5A version which was successful as long as you could deal with the additional heat.  The PSU even featured in the Nascom 3 factory built machines with its 5V regulator mounted on the rear panel.
