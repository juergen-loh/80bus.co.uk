Nascom 1 Buffer Board - Nascom 1 to 80-BUS

Description

The original Nascom 1 was not capable of driving the 80-BUS.  This card acts as an interface between a Nascom 1 and the 80-BUS.  This functionality was later built into the Nascom 2 computer.
