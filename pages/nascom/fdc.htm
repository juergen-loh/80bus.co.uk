Lucas / Nascom - Floppy Disk Controller Card

Description

Lucas released a floppy disk controller card based on the Western Digital 1793 FDC chip.  The board was capable of driving both 5.25 and 8 inch drives, but was only supplied with 5.25 units.  Originally it was thought that this was a System 80 component but was actually a new design.

It should be noted that some late Lucas Nascom machines had a hard drive option.  Since the FDC has no SASI interface, a none Lucas card must have been used.  No conclusive proof exists, but this was thought to be a MAP-80 FDC as this could be configured to look like a Lucas FDC but with a SASI interface.

Technical Details

  • Capable of Driving 5.25 and 8 Inch Drives
  • 1793 FDC Design
  • Up to 4 Drives
