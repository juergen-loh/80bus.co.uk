Nascom 1 - 3A PSU

Description

The original Nascom 1 was supplied with a kit for a 3A PSU if required.  This was superseded fairly quickly by a similar 3A PSU that became standard for the Nascom 2 as well.  Due to this, this item is fairly rare.
