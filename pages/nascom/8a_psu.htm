Lucas / Nascom  - 8A PSU

Description

An 8A PSU was produced after the rescue of Nascom by Lucas Logic. This was capable of powering just about any standard Nascom setup.  It was slightly odd in that a linear unit was designed rather than a switch mode one.  A kit was available for those who enjoyed playing with mains voltages.
