Winchester / SASI / ST506 Based Hard Drives

Both Gemini and Lucas / Nascom supported the SASI interface, however they used ST506 based hard drives.  This was achieved by the use of a third party controller card, usually a Xebec S1410A. However it is known that Gemini also used an Adaptec unit (ACB4000A).  These controller cards operated by plugging into a connector on the floppy controller in the system.  For Gemini this was either a GM829 or GM849.  For Nascom / Lucas there was no available controller card with the necessary port.  This was solved by the clever use of the MAP-80 MPI card.  This card could be configured to look like either a GM829 or the Nascom / Lucas controller. However, since it had a connector for a hard drive controller, it could be used to replace the original Nascom / Lucas FDC and to add the required interface at the same time.
