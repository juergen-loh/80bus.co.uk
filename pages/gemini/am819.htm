AM819 - Speech Card

Description

This half size card was originally developed by Aarfon around the National Semiconductor Digitalker chipset.  The card has a vocabulary of 120 words held in a ROM so it was feasible to customize the known words.  It should be noted that the 8K ROM used to hold the speech information was the same type employed to hold the Nascom ROM Basic.
