GM849 / GM849A - Advanced Floppy Disk Controller

Description

This Improved FDC card was introduced to replace the GM829.   Improvements included support for 3 inch drives and tape streamers with the GM849A variant.  It is generally backwards compatible with the GM809, GM829 cards.

Technical Details

  • Based around the Western Digital WD2793 controller
  • Capable of driving 3,  5.25 and 8 inch drives
