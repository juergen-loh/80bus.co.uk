BE847 - Arithmetic Processor Card

Description

This card was designed to speed up floating point calculation via the use of a dedicated maths processor and was originally supplied by Belectra.  Hisoft Pascal 5 supported use of this card directly but little other software was developed to make use of the facilities offered. 

One odd detail is that the card is half size, being only 4 inches deep.

Technical Details

  • HSA-88B processor
  • Small card, 8 x 4 inches
  • Very nice shade of blue

Documentation
