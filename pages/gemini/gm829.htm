GM829 - Floppy Disk Controller

Description

This Improved FDC card was introduced to replace the GM809.   Improvements included support for faster stepping drives as well as hard drives via a SASI interface connector.

Technical Details

  • Based around the Western Digital WD1797 controller
  • Capable of driving both 5.25 and 8 inch drives
