GM816 - Multi I/O Card

Description

This I/O Board, RTC, CTC, 3*PIO was Originally developed by Quantum.  It is generally similar to the Nascom I/O board but with the ability to take piggy-back boards to expand internally. The only items made available where the GM818 serial board and the GM663 prototyping board.

Technical Details

  • 1 x Real time clock
  • 1 x Z80 CTC
  • 3 x Z80 PIO
