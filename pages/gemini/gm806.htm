GM806 - Supermum - Nascom 1 Buffer Board

Description

The Nascom 1 is not able to produce the NAS-BUS signal set.  To do this Nascom produced a buffer board but this suffered from a number of problems.  Gemini in turn produced a Buffer Board  / 5A PSU combination in the form of a kit. The Buffer Board also includes a reset jump circuit.

Technical Details

  • 5A PSU
  • Nascom 1 to NAS-BUS conversion
