GM870 - Modem Card

Description

This card was the only modem card that went into production for 80-Bus systems.  For the time it was a very advanced device featuring multiple baud rates, auto answering and duplex communication.

Technical Details

  • 300 baud full duplex
  • 1200 / 75 baud asymmetric duplex
  • Auto dial
  • Auto answer
